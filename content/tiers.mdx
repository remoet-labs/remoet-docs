# Tiers

remoet.dev has three tiers. The free tier is the right starting point for most developers — you can connect an AI agent, star companies, and build against the REST API without paying anything.

## Plan comparison

| Feature | Free | Pro | Max |
|---|---|---|---|
| Price | $0 | $15/mo | $29/mo |
| Star caps (companies you follow) | 10 | 30 | 75 |
| Unstars per month | 5 | 15 | unlimited |
| MCP requests / day | 30 | 150 | unlimited |
| REST API requests / day | 300 | 5,000 | unlimited |
| Job data freshness | 1 week delay | live | live |
| Link trees | 1 | 10 | unlimited |

Prices are inclusive of tax (Swedish VAT customers pay $15 total, not $15 + tax). USD-only for now.

## A few things to know

**Starring is free.** No budget cost per star — you only consume budget when you *unstar*. This prevents cycling exploits where you could trivially churn through companies.

**Unstars reset monthly.** Free gets 5, Pro gets 15, Max is unlimited. The monthly clock starts when you upgrade and resets at the same day of the month.

**MCP and REST quotas are separate.** Daily quota for MCP doesn't drain your REST budget and vice versa. Both reset at 00:00 UTC.

**Job data delay on Free.** Free users see jobs that are at least a week old. Pro and Max see jobs as soon as the scraper publishes them. Useful if you care about being first to apply.

**Plan changes apply immediately.** Upgrade via [www.remoet.dev/settings](https://www.remoet.dev/settings). Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period (Stripe handles the proration).

## Refunds and disputes

We process payments through Stripe. Refunds available from the Stripe Customer Portal (linked from /settings). If something looks wrong on your account, refund first and we'll sort out the dispute — chargebacks are expensive on both sides.

## What's the same across tiers

- API key generation, 5 keys per account, key revocation
- The full set of MCP tools and REST endpoints (quotas differ, not the surface)
- Profile data export (the <Link href="/full">/user/full</Link> endpoint works for all tiers)
- OAuth flow for Claude Web custom connectors
